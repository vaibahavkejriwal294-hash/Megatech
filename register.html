Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Students from classes 8-12 and college students can participate. Some events have specific eligibility criteria mentioned in the event details.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes! You can register for multiple events as long as they don't overlap in timing. Check the schedule to plan accordingly.

Is accommodation provided?

Accommodation is not included in the registration fee but can be arranged on request for outstation participants at additional cost.

What should I bring?

Bring your school ID, registration confirmation, laptop (for coding events), and any specific equipment required for your events.

Is there a refund policy?

Cancellations made 7 days before the event get 50% refund. No refunds for cancellations within 7 days of the event.

How do team registrations work?

Each team member needs to register individually and mention the same team name. One person should be designated as the team leader.
